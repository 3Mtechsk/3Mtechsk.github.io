SAP Business Intelligence

SAP NetWeaver Business Intelligence, a component of the SAP NetWeaver platform, is a powerful tool for the identification, integration, and analysis of disparate business data from heterogeneous sources. It includes data-warehousing features, reporting and analysis tools, best-practice models, business-analysis applications, and administrative resources.

With its superior analytical capabilities, SAP Business Intelligence generates and delivers tailored business insight and expertise for any purpose enabling better informed management decisions.

More and more companies realize that business insight is needed anywhere in their organizations – from administrator to executive level. Consequently, business insight components could become an integral part of all business scenarios as companies move to service oriented architectures. Additionally, the information-centric economy companies’ face today has led to a massive and continuous growth in data volume and complexity. An organization’s IT infrastructure becomes the critical element for the identification, collection, analysis and accurate provision of relevant information. To cope with the resulting intense and unpredictable data loads and the increasing number of users, the IT landscape must be highly flexible and reliable. Successful companies need IT resources that are dynamically scalable to adapt to changing demand and usage scenarios, ensure a stable performance as well as real-time responses, and are highly integrated.

BW services provided by our company include

  • Data warehouse strategy
  • Business case development
  • Assessment and design of technical architecture and infrastructure
  • Enterprise data modeling
  • BPS integration services
  • Implementation support for standard business content
  • Custom-designed BW solutions
  • Analysis of query performance
  • Support for inclusion of non-R/3 data in the BW
  • Training assistance

mySAP ERP

In order to compete in today’s dynamic business world, successful companies around the globe rely on mySAP Enterprise Resource Planning. mySAP ERP helps organizations to become more efficient and productive and prepares them for change and sustainable growth. Based on SAP NetWeaver, SAP’s open integration and application platform, it tightly integrates information and streamlines processes.

Our company offers a comprehensive solution portfolio that helps customers maximize the value of their mySAP ERP installation. Based on our unique experience and competence in the SAP environment and in close collaboration with SAP, we build complete and integrated solutions that enable change and innovation in your company and that are tailored to your specific needs and business objectives.

mySAP ERP is very attractive for companies upgrading from an older SAP R/3 releases as well as for completely new SAP customers. It also offers organizations with advanced installations impressive business insight capabilities. Therefore our company offers specially designed solutions for each of these scenarios.

Solutions and services

Gathering the right information in the right time is one of the most important qualifications for successful business activities of every organization. Value of this requirement is strategically significant and depends on the growing size of organization. Well prepared and reliable operational information system assists to make right decisions throughout all organizational levels and mainly saves money in order to be effectively invested in development of the other core activities of the organization. In 3MTech we fully understand the importance of early and quality information accessible on the all organizational levels. Therefore our core business is devoted to the production of software solutions and providing expert services aimed to achieving easy and quick process of information gathering and efficient work with them.

Custom development

Our company focuses in the custom development mainly on customers with specific demands, which are not satisfied with presently available standard solutions. These solutions usually do not fulfill all customer’s requirements and offer such functionality that the customer cannot fully utilize. Therefore our goal is to create a solution that solves all specific customer’s needs and its quality guarantees effective and correct operations.

Consulting services

Our company offers its consulting services to all organizations considering development of new or innovation of their current information system solution. The goal of these services, in close contact with the customer, is added value gained from ability of better information exploitation.

Assistance services

Constant maintenance about our products we guarantee by support services focused on solving problems with run of applications and guidance for application usage. Assistance services are accessible on various levels during the regular working hours or out of the regular working hours.

Outsourcing

The outsourcing services of our company focus on taking responsibility over development, upgrading, management and maintenance of software applications providing organizations with informational functions. Our professionals are capable of outsourcing every application regardless of its size - standard package application or custom solution developed by any supplier.
